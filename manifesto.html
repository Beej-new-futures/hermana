Hermana is a Somerset-based collective founded by artists Georgina Gordon-Smith and Daljit Kaur, specialising in bespoke paint finishes that elevate surfaces into refined design features. Our work brings depth, material richness, and a timeless quality to interiors, with careful attention to light, tone, and atmosphere. We also offer restoration and colour matching, with sustainability at the core of our materials and process.
