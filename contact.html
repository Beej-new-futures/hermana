A percentage of all Hermana proceeds is donated to The Circle, a global feminist organisation founded in 2008 by award-winning singer-songwriter and activist Annie Lennox. The Circle works with women and allies around the world to advance equality, improve safety, and support a living wage for women and girls facing gender-based violence and economic injustice.
